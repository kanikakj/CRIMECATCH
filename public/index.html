About Project


CRIMECATCH is used to discover criminals, prevent crime, and apprehend wanted perpetrators. As the world facilitates, the number of crimes goes through the roof, making fingerprint detection more difficult since thieves are sophisticated enough to obliterate evidence. CRIMECATCH assists in detecting criminals around you in order to provide you with a secure environment to live in and to avoid crime. Detection assists you in determining who is secure to let into your safe environment.
